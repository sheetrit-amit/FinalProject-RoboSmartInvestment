The System

Three layers of intelligence

Step 01

Risk Classification

A Decision Tree classifier trained on 200 companies assigns every stock in our 1,001-company universe a risk label — Low, Med-Low, Medium, Med-High, or High — using volatility, beta, and six financial ratios.

Decision Tree · scikit-learn
Step 02

Markowitz Optimisation

Stocks matching your risk level enter a mean-variance optimiser. It scans the Efficient Frontier and returns the portfolio with the maximum Sharpe Ratio — the best risk-adjusted return maths can find.

Modern Portfolio Theory · SciPy
Step 03

AI Synthesis

LLM-generated fundamental scores from quarterly SEC filings are merged with the Markowitz weights. A final AI model produces a human-readable investment thesis for every position in the basket.

LLM · OpenRouter · Free Tier
